How it works

From financial files to CFO-level insight in three steps.

Once the analysis is complete, we send you a practical financial report with insights, forecasts, risks, and recommended next steps for your business.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

The things business owners ask us most before sending their first set of files.

Is my financial data safe?
Yes. Only the team preparing your report sees your files, we never sell your information, and you can ask us to delete it at any time. We also ask that you don't send passwords, bank logins, or Social Security numbers — we don't need them. See our Privacy Policy and Security page for details.
What if my books are messy or incomplete?
That's exactly what the Financial Cleanup is for. Send whatever you have — we'll organize your income and expense categories, flag what's missing or unclear, and tell you what to fix before deeper analysis. You don't need perfect books to start.
How long until I get my report?
We confirm the turnaround with you as soon as we receive your files — it depends on the service and how much data you send. Most reports are delivered within a few business days, and your finished report arrives straight in your inbox.
Do you replace my accountant or CPA?
No. FinsightCFO provides financial analysis and decision support that works alongside your CPA, bookkeeper, or tax advisor. Our reports are not accounting, tax, or legal advice — they help you understand your numbers and decide what to do next.
Is this software I log into?
No — there's no dashboard or login. FinsightCFO is a hands-on service: you send us your financial files, our team analyzes them with AI-assisted tools, and we email you a finished CFO-style report.
What do I actually need to send?
Whatever you have — profit & loss statements, balance sheets, bank statements, revenue and expense reports, or QuickBooks/Excel/CSV exports. See the full list under What you can submit.
How do I get started and pay?
Pick a service under Pricing or start your report. We'll confirm the scope and payment details with you before any work begins — no surprises.
