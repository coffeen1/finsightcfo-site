002 / Why we started

Small businesses deserve better financial clarity.

We started FinsightCFO because we kept seeing the same problem: small business owners were building real companies, serving real customers, and making important decisions without the financial support that larger organizations take for granted.

Most owners have the data somewhere, bank statements, accounting exports, payroll reports, expense records, revenue reports, and spreadsheets. But having the data is not the same as understanding what it means.

Large companies can afford CFO teams, consultants, analysts, and custom financial models. Small businesses usually cannot. We believe AI can help close that gap.

We use AI heavily in our own work to research faster, structure analysis, organize financial information, test assumptions, and turn scattered numbers into clearer business decisions. FinsightCFO combines AI-assisted analysis with practical CFO-style thinking so small businesses can receive useful reports, forecasts, risks, and next steps without needing a full finance department.

This is about helping businesses build something good, powerful, and lasting.

Growing up around small businesses, Bryce saw firsthand how frustrating it can be for owners to manage cash flow, expenses, payroll, and planning while also trying to run and grow the company.
