The why

Why we started FinsightCFO.

We started FinsightCFO because we believe small businesses are one of the most important forces in the economy. They create jobs, build communities, serve real customers, and give people the chance to turn an idea into something meaningful.

But too often, the owners building those businesses are forced to make major decisions without clear financial visibility. They have bank accounts, accounting software, invoices, payroll, expenses, and reports, but not always a simple way to understand what the numbers are saying or what decision should come next.

Large companies can afford CFO teams, consultants, analysts, and custom financial models. Small businesses usually cannot. That gap creates a real disadvantage for founders and owners who are trying to grow responsibly.

We saw AI as a way to help close that gap. We use AI heavily ourselves, and we believe it can make high-quality financial support faster, clearer, and more affordable. FinsightCFO combines the financial files you send us, practical forecasting, AI-assisted analysis, and CFO-style review so business owners can understand their numbers and act with confidence.

This is not just about software. It is about helping businesses make something good and powerful. Better numbers lead to better decisions. Better decisions help companies protect cash, hire at the right time, grow sustainably, and build something that lasts.

The founders

Two operators building for operators.

Bryce Armstrong
Co-Founder

Bryce Armstrong studied Economics at Arizona State University, where he developed a strong foundation in markets, business decision-making, and how financial systems shape growth. Growing up around small businesses, Bryce saw firsthand how difficult it can be for owners to manage cash flow, expenses, payroll, and planning while also trying to serve customers and grow the company.

That experience shaped his view that small businesses do not just need more software. They need clearer financial guidance, better operating systems, and access to the kind of CFO-level thinking that larger companies rely on. Bryce uses AI heavily to research, analyze, organize information, and build faster workflows that turn scattered financial data into practical next steps for business owners.

At FinsightCFO, Bryce focuses on customer strategy, business operations, financial clarity, and making AI-powered finance support approachable for real small business owners.
