Bryce Armstrong studied Economics at Arizona State University, where he developed a strong foundation in markets, business decision-making, and how financial systems shape growth. Growing up around small businesses, Bryce saw firsthand how frustrating it can be for owners to manage cash flow, expenses, payroll, and planning while also trying to run and grow the company.

That experience shaped his belief that small businesses need more than software. They need clear financial guidance, better systems, and access to the kind of CFO-level thinking that larger companies rely on. Bryce uses AI heavily to research, analyze, organize information, and build workflows that turn messy financial data into practical next steps for business owners.

At FinsightCFO, Bryce focuses on customer strategy, business operations, financial clarity, and making AI-powered finance support useful for real small business owners.

“I grew up seeing how much pressure small business owners carry. We want to give them the financial clarity and confidence that larger companies get from full finance teams.”

002 / Why we started

Small businesses deserve better financial clarity.

01

Owners were building real companies and making big decisions without the financial support larger organizations take for granted.

02

Most owners have the data somewhere — bank statements, accounting exports, payroll, expense and revenue reports, spreadsheets. But having the data is not the same as understanding what it means. Large companies can afford CFO teams, analysts, and custom models. Small businesses usually cannot.

03

We believe AI can close that gap. We use it heavily to research faster, structure analysis, and turn scattered numbers into clearer decisions — pairing AI-assisted analysis with practical CFO-style thinking, so you get useful reports, forecasts, and next steps without a full finance department.

This is about helping businesses build something good, powerful, and lasting.
